Topic D Equations

D.1 Gravitational Fields

Standard level and higher level:
F = Gm₁m₂/r²
g = F/m = GM/r²
Additional higher level:
Eₚ = -Gm₁m₂/r
Vg = -GM/r
g = -ΔVg/Δr
W = mΔVg
v_esc = √(2GM/r)
v_orbital = √(GM/r)

D.2 Electric and Magnetic Fields

Standard level and higher level:
F = kq₁q₂/r² where k = 1/(4πε₀)
E = F/q
E = V/d
Additional higher level:
Eₚ = kq₁q₂/r
Ve = kQ/r
E = -ΔVe/Δr
W = qΔVe

D.3 Motion in Electromagnetic Fields

Standard level and higher level:
F = qvB sin θ
F = BIL sin θ
F/L = μ₀I₁I₂/(2πr)
